Services Offered

  • Information & Awareness
  • Support Services

Information & Awareness

  1. Myth-Busting Content-addressing misconceptions about mental health.
  2. Self-Help Toolkits-downloadable checklists, journaling prompts, and coping exercises.
  3. Awareness Campaigns-themed around events like World Mental Health Day or Suicide Prevention Month

Support Services

  1. Peer Support Forums-safe, moderated spaces to share experiences.
  2. Support Group Listings-local and online groups for various conditions.
  3. Live Chat / Helpline Integration-instant connection to trained listeners or crisis lines.
Advocacy & Community Engagement
  • Story Sharing Platform – real-life recovery stories and personal journeys.
  • Petitions & Policy Advocacy – engaging visitors in mental health rights initiatives.
  • Workshops & Webinars – on topics like managing anxiety, workplace well-being, or youth mental health.
